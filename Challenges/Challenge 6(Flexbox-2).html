MY TOP MOVIE PICKS

INTERSTELLAR

Christopher Nolan’s "Interstellar," about astronauts traveling to the other end of the galaxy to find a new home to replace humanity’s despoiled home-world, is frantically busy and earsplittingly loud. It uses booming music to jack up the excitement level of scenes that might not otherwise excite. It features characters shoveling exposition at each other for almost three hours, and a few of those characters have no character to speak of: they’re mouthpieces for techno-babble and philosophical debate. And for all of the director’s activism on behalf of shooting on film, the tactile beauty of the movie’s 35mm and 65mm textures isn't matched by a sense of composition. The camera rarely tells the story in Nolan's movies. More often it illustrates the screenplay, and there are points in this one where I felt as if I was watching the most expensive NBC pilot ever made.

AVENGERS ENDGAME

Avengers Endgame is the final chapter of Marvel's epic saga that began with Iron Man in 2008. The film follows the surviving heroes of the Avengers, who lost half of their allies and friends to Thanos' snap in Infinity War. They devise a plan to travel back in time and collect the six Infinity Stones before Thanos can use them to wipe out half of all life. Along the way, they encounter their past selves, old enemies and allies, and face the consequences of their actions. They manage to bring back the snapped heroes and engage in a climactic battle with Thanos and his army. In the end, Iron Man sacrifices himself to use the Stones and destroy Thanos, while Captain America decides to live out his life with Peggy Carter and passes on his shield to Falcon.

KGF CHAPTER 2

KGF CHAPTER 2 is a Kannada-language action film that continues the story of Rocky, who has taken over the Kolar Gold Fields after killing Garuda. He faces new enemies in Adheera, a ruthless rival who wants the gold mines, and Ramika Sen, the Prime Minister of India who wants to arrest him. Rocky must fight for his survival and his ambition to rule the world.K.G.F: Chapter 1’s story begins showing journalist Anand Ingalagi (Anant Nag) whose book “El Dorado” detailing the events in the Kolar Gold Fields during the period 1951 to 2018 got banned by the Indian government.

KANTARA

Kantara is a fictional story inspired by the relationship shared between forests and human beings. The story takes place over almost two centuries and depicts Supreme God Vishnu's third incarnation on earth called Varaha avatar. The film begins with a King’s story in 1847 and then cuts to 1970 when the hero’s father performs Kolam and mysteriously disappears into forests. In 1990, when the story is set, the hero is haunted by dreams. He sees god coming in Varaha roopam3. The hero of “Kantara” belongs to the Bhoota Kola family.

INCEPTION

Inception is a sci-fi thriller about a team of experts who use a device called the PASIV to enter people's dreams and manipulate them. The team is led by Dom Cobb, a skilled extractor who is haunted by the memory of his deceased wife, Mal. Cobb is offered a chance to return to his children in the US, who he has not seen for years, if he can perform a rare and risky task: inception. Inception is planting an idea in someone's mind, rather than stealing it. Cobb's target is Robert Fischer, the heir of a powerful business empire. Cobb and his team must create a complex dream within a dream within a dream, and plant the idea that Fischer should dissolve his father's company. Along the way, they face many obstacles, such as Fischer's trained subconscious defenses, Mal's unpredictable appearances, and the danger of losing themselves in the dream world. The movie ends with a cliffhanger, as Cobb spins a top that was Mal's totem, a device that helps him distinguish reality from dreams, and walks away to embrace his children, without checking if the top falls or keeps spinning.

BAHUBALI 2

The film tells the story of Amarendra Baahubali, the prince of Mahishmati, who falls in love with Devasena, the princess of Kuntala. His brother Bhallaladeva, who wants to be the king, plots against him with his father Bijjaladeva and makes Kattappa, a loyal warrior, kill him. Years later, Amarendra's son Mahendra Baahubali learns the truth and avenges his father's death by defeating Bhallaladeva in a final battle. Indian Telugu-language epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli, who co-wrote the film with V. Vijayendra Prasad. It was produced by Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni under the banner Arka Media Works. The film was produced in Tollywood, the centre of Telugu language films in India, and was filmed in both Telugu and Tamil languages.
